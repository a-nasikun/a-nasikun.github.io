Mapping high school selectivity in Yogyakarta

Every year, thousands of Yogyakarta middle-schoolers compete for seats at 11 public high schools (SMA Negeri) through five admission tracks — Zonasi Radius, Zonasi Reguler, Afirmasi, Prestasi, and Pindah Tugas. This dashboard reads the official PPDB results to show how selective each school and track actually was, and which middle schools (SMP/MTs) feed into which high schools.

How students flow: SMP/MTs → SMA

Each large circle is one of the 11 SMA Negeri, colored individually and sized by how many students it accepted. Each small square is a middle school (SMP/MTs), sized by how many students it sent on to any of the 11 schools — all drawn in the same neutral color, since the square's size is what matters here, not its identity. An edge's thickness and tint reflect how many students made that specific journey. Drag nodes, scroll to zoom, and hover to trace a school's feeders.
